How it works
Upload weekly or any date range — duplicates are skipped automatically
1 Export sales weekly or monthly from Odoo as CSV. Use the 'Dashboard - Rev' template under Sales Reporting. Always export the complete day — do not export current day sales to avoid duplicated exports.
2 Drag & drop the file above — no renaming needed
3 Data loads to odoo_sales and inserts into transactions automatically
4 Refresh the dashboard — new sales appears in all filters
⚠ This upload deduplicates on Order Date + Product/Barcode + Order + Untaxed Total. However, please do your best in uploading only up to previous day's sales to avoid any complications.
